# Social Media Checklist for College Students
## The Do’s
- Privacy & Safety : Do audit your privacy settings regularly to control who sees your posts

- Professionalism	: Do use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with professors and alumni

- Engagement : Do think for 60 seconds before posting or commenting during an argument

- Fact-Checking	: Do verify information from a credible source before hitting the share button

- Consistency	: Do keep your "About Me" sections updated and professional
## The Don’ts
- Privacy & Safety : Don’t share your real-time location or sensitive data like your student ID number

- Professionalism	: Don’t post content showing illegal activities or excessive partying

- Engagement :	Don’t engage with "trolls" or participate in cyberbullying and online drama

- Fact-Checking :	Don’t spread unverified rumors about campus events or individuals

- Consistency	: Don’t use an unprofessional profile picture (keep it clear and friendly)
